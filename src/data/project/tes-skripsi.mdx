Tes Skripsi is a thesis examination management system designed for universities to organize and streamline the entire thesis testing process. The application manages student registration for thesis examinations, examiner assignments, scheduling, and score recording — all in a centralized digital platform.

The system reduces administrative bottlenecks in academic departments by automating scheduling and notification workflows. Faculty coordinators can track the progress of each student's examination from registration to final result, ensuring a more organized and transparent evaluation process.
