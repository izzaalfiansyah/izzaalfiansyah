Puskesmas is a community health center (Puskesmas) service application developed for Elly Uyo Adipura health facility. The system manages patient registration, medical records, doctor schedules, and service queues — streamlining healthcare delivery at the community level.

By digitizing patient workflows, the application reduces waiting times and administrative overhead for health workers. Staff can access patient history quickly, manage consultations efficiently, and generate health service reports — ultimately improving the quality of care provided to the local community.
