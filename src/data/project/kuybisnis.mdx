KuyBisnis is a web-based business tracking and entrepreneurship planning platform built for student groups. The application helps users plan their entrepreneurial ventures, monitor business activities, and evaluate the results of their business operations — all in one place.

Designed to nurture student entrepreneurship, KuyBisnis provides structured tools for recording revenues, expenses, and milestones, as well as tracking business growth over time. It encourages a data-driven approach to running a small business from the ground up, making it ideal for academic entrepreneurship programs and competitions.
