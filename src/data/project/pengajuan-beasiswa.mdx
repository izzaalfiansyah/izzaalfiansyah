Pengajuan Beasiswa is a scholarship application system developed for Poltas (Politeknik Tegal Selatan). The platform enables students to submit scholarship applications online by filling out forms and uploading supporting documents, eliminating the need for physical submission and manual processing.

Scholarship administrators can review applications, assess eligibility criteria, and manage the selection process through the system. The application ensures a fair, transparent, and efficient scholarship distribution process, helping deserving students gain access to financial support for their education.
