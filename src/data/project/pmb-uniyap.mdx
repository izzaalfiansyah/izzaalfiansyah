PMB Uniyap is a new student registration application developed for Universitas Yapis Papua (UNIYAP). The platform digitizes the university's admissions process — allowing prospective students to register online, submit required documents, and track their application status from anywhere.

University administrators can review applications, manage enrollment quotas per study program, and communicate decisions to applicants through the system. By moving admissions online, the application makes higher education more accessible for students across Papua and surrounding regions.
