E-SPP is a school tuition payment management application that digitalizes the collection and tracking of student SPP (Sumbangan Pembinaan Pendidikan) fees. The system allows finance staff to record incoming payments, issue digital receipts, and monitor the payment status of every student in the school.

Parents can check their child's payment history and outstanding balances online, while administrators can generate monthly and annual financial reports with ease. The application improves financial transparency, reduces manual data entry errors, and ensures a more organized payment collection process.
