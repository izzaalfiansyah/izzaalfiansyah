Sewa Kamar is a room rental management application that connects room seekers with property owners in a streamlined digital platform. Prospective tenants can browse available rooms, view details and photos, and submit rental requests — all without visiting in person first.

Property owners and managers can list their rooms, manage tenant bookings, track payment schedules, and handle lease agreements through the system. The application simplifies the rental process for both parties, making room hunting and property management more efficient and transparent.
