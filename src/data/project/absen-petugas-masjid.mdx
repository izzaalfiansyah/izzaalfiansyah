Absen Petugas Masjid is an attendance management application built to track the presence of mosque staff and officers, such as imams, muezzins, and cleaning personnel. The system digitizes the daily attendance recording process, replacing physical sign-in sheets with a structured web-based solution.

Mosque administrators can monitor staff attendance in real time, generate attendance reports, and identify patterns of absence. The application ensures accountability and proper scheduling for mosque operations, contributing to a well-organized and consistent worship environment.
