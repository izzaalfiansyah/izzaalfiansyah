Aset Desa Brebes is a land and asset management application developed for PT MG Intermediatama to digitize and manage property records in the Brebes region. The system provides a structured database of land parcels and assets, replacing manual ledgers with a searchable and auditable digital registry.

The application helps the organization maintain accurate and up-to-date records of their land holdings, track ownership details, and generate reports for administrative and legal purposes — improving transparency and efficiency in asset governance.
