LostInfo is a digital platform for reporting and searching lost and found items, making the spread of information faster and more effective. Users can post reports of lost belongings or found items, and the platform intelligently compares them to suggest potential matches — helping owners reunite with their property more quickly.

One of its standout features is the item comparison engine, which cross-references lost and found listings to surface similar items. Additionally, the platform includes an auction feature for unclaimed found items, providing a fair and transparent resolution mechanism when no owner comes forward.
