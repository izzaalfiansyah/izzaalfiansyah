Siphuda is a legal product inventory system developed for the Secretariat of the Regional Legislative Council (DPRD) of Trenggalek Regency. The system provides a structured digital repository for cataloging, searching, and managing regional legal documents and regulations produced by the council.

By replacing manual filing systems with a searchable database, Siphuda improves the accessibility and integrity of official legal records. Government staff can quickly retrieve specific regulations, track document revisions, and generate reports — ensuring better compliance, transparency, and governance within the regional legislative body.
