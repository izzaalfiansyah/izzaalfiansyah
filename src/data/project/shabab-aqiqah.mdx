Shabab Aqiqah is a management application for handling qurban (sacrificial animal) and aqiqah (birth celebration) orders. The platform allows customers to place orders for religious slaughter services, select animal types and quantities, and specify delivery or distribution preferences.

Administrators can manage the full order lifecycle — from intake and animal preparation to distribution and reporting. The system brings efficiency and accountability to a community-based religious service, helping operators serve more customers during peak seasons such as Eid al-Adha.
