Direktori Biodata is an employee profile and biodata management application that serves as a centralized directory for worker information within an organization. The system stores personal details, contact information, job roles, and other relevant employee data in a structured and searchable format.

HR teams and managers can quickly look up employee profiles, update records, and export data for reporting purposes. The application improves organizational efficiency by replacing scattered spreadsheets and manual files with a unified, easy-to-manage digital directory.
