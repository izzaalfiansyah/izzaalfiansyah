Jurnal Mengajar is a digital teaching journal application developed for SMKN 2 Jember. The system allows teachers to record and manage their daily teaching activities, including subject matter covered, attendance notes, and class observations — all in a structured and easily retrievable format.

By digitizing the teaching log process, the platform helps school administrators monitor instructional consistency and compliance, while giving teachers a convenient way to document and review their teaching history throughout the academic year.
