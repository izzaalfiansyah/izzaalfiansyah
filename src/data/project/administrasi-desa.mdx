Administrasi Desa is a village administration application that serves as a digital bridge between village residents and local government. The platform allows residents to submit complaints, requests, and reports directly to village officials, replacing the need for in-person visits for routine administrative matters.

Village administrators can manage and respond to incoming submissions, track the status of each request, and maintain a transparent record of community interactions. The system promotes participatory governance and improves the responsiveness of local government to residents' needs.
