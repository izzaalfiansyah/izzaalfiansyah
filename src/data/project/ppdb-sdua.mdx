PPDB SDUA is a new student enrollment application developed for SD Ummu Aiman (SDUA). The system digitizes the entire registration process — from online form submission and document uploads to registration status tracking and enrollment confirmation — making it more accessible for prospective students and their parents.

School administrators can manage incoming applications, review submitted documents, and communicate acceptance decisions through the platform. The application reduces long queues and paperwork associated with traditional enrollment processes, providing a smoother experience for both families and school staff.
