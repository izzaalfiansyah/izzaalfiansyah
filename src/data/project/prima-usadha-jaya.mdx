Prima Usadha Jaya is a workshop service management application designed to streamline the operational workflow of an automotive repair shop. The system handles service job orders, vehicle data, technician assignments, spare parts inventory, and billing — all in one integrated platform.

By digitizing the workshop's day-to-day operations, the application helps owners and service advisors track repair progress in real time, manage customer vehicles efficiently, and produce accurate service invoices. It reduces manual errors and improves the overall service experience for both staff and customers.
