E-Kuisioner is an alumni tracking and survey application designed to help educational institutions stay connected with their graduates. Alumni can register, update their current employment status, and fill out questionnaires — providing valuable data for institutional accreditation and program improvement.

The platform enables academic departments to collect structured feedback from graduates about the relevance of their education to real-world careers. The resulting data supports decision-making for curriculum development and helps institutions demonstrate graduate outcomes to accreditation bodies.
