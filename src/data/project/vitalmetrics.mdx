Vitalmetrics is an Internet of Things (IoT) based BMI (Body Mass Index) calculator platform that connects physical health monitoring devices with a web application. Users can measure their weight and height through IoT-enabled sensors, and the platform automatically computes and records their BMI in real time.

The system provides a digital health tracking experience by storing historical BMI data and displaying trends over time. It bridges hardware and software seamlessly, making health monitoring more interactive, data-driven, and accessible for everyday users.
