Prima Store is an online marketplace application built specifically for students at SMK PGRI 05 Jember. The platform allows students to buy and sell school-related goods — such as uniforms, stationery, and other supplies — within their own school community.

The application provides a safe, school-centered e-commerce environment where students can manage product listings, process orders, and handle transactions. It serves as both a practical tool for student needs and an educational experience in digital commerce and entrepreneurship.
