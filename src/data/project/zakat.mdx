Zakat is a zakat (Islamic almsgiving) management application developed for the village of Ujong Gunong Rayeuk. The system facilitates the collection, recording, and distribution of zakat funds — ensuring transparency and accountability in the management of this religious obligation at the community level.

Village zakat administrators can track contributions from individual households, manage recipient eligibility, and document distribution records. The application modernizes what was traditionally a manual process, making it easier to manage zakat funds fairly and in accordance with Islamic guidelines.
