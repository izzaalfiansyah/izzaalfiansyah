Aset Tanah Polda is a land asset management system developed for the regional police (Polda) to digitize and manage land ownership and territorial data. The system integrates GIS (Geographic Information System) capabilities to visualize land parcels on an interactive map, making it easier to track, verify, and manage government-owned land assets.

The platform provides a centralized and structured way to store land documents, boundaries, and ownership records — reducing reliance on manual paperwork and improving data accuracy for official reporting and asset verification purposes.
