Absensi QRCode is a QR code-based attendance management system that modernizes the check-in process for schools, organizations, or workplaces. Each user is assigned a unique QR code, which is scanned at attendance points to instantly record their presence — eliminating the need for manual sign-in sheets or time-consuming roll calls.

The system automatically logs attendance timestamps, generates attendance reports, and tracks punctuality and absences over time. Administrators can monitor attendance data in real time through a dashboard, making it easier to manage workforce or student presence with accuracy and efficiency.
