Pinjam Sarana is a school facility borrowing management application that allows students and staff to request the use of school equipment and facilities — such as projectors, sports gear, and lab tools — through an online system. The platform tracks item availability, borrowing schedules, and return deadlines in real time.

School administrators can manage facility requests, approve or reject bookings, and monitor the condition and usage history of each item. The application helps prevent scheduling conflicts and ensures that school resources are used responsibly and efficiently.
