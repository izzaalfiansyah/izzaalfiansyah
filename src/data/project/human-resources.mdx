Human Resources is a comprehensive digital platform designed to manage and streamline HR operations within organizations. The system handles the full employee lifecycle — from recruitment and onboarding to performance tracking, leave management, training and development, and retention analysis — all within a single unified interface.

Built with Vue.js on the frontend and Laravel on the backend, the platform empowers HR teams to make data-driven decisions through intuitive dashboards, automated workflows, and detailed analytics. It reduces administrative overhead while improving transparency and employee engagement across the organization.
