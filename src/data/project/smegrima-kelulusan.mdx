Smegrima Kelulusan is a graduation check application developed for SMK PGRI 05 Jember students. The platform allows students to quickly verify their graduation status online by entering their student identification number, eliminating the need to visit the school in person for this information.

School administrators can update and manage graduation data directly through the system, ensuring that results are published accurately and promptly. The application simplifies the announcement process and provides students and parents with convenient, real-time access to graduation information.
