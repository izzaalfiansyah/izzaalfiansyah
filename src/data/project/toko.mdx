Toko is a general-purpose store sales application designed to manage product inventory and sales transactions for retail businesses. The system allows shop owners to manage their product catalog, record incoming orders, and track sales performance through a simple and intuitive interface.

Built with Laravel, the application supports day-to-day store operations including stock management, transaction history, and basic reporting. It provides small and medium-sized retailers with a reliable digital tool to organize their business operations and keep accurate records of their sales activities.
